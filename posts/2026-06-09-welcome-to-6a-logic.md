# Welcome to the 6A Logic Blog

For years, we've watched small and mid-sized businesses chase AI tools that never quite deliver. Not because the technology is broken — but because the data underneath is.

AI doesn't fail in demos. It fails in production, when it runs into disconnected CRMs, manual spreadsheets, and systems that don't talk to each other. That's the problem we solve.

## What This Blog Is For

This blog exists to be useful. We'll write about:

- **The real reasons AI projects stall** — and what it actually takes to fix them
- **Practical automation patterns** for operations teams without dedicated engineering staff
- **Case studies** from the kinds of businesses we work with
- **Technical explainers** on MCP, data normalization, and workflow orchestration — written for operators, not engineers

## Who We're Writing For

If you're running a business with somewhere between 5 and 200 employees, juggling five or more tools, and wondering why your data never quite adds up — this is for you.

You don't need to be technical. You need to know what's possible, what it costs, and whether it's worth it.

We'll try to answer all three.
